How to order a stretch ceiling?
Ordering a stretch ceiling in ALFASTYLE is very easy - just 4 steps.
The first step - consultation You can get a consultation from a personal manager by phone:
The second step - measurement A technologist-measurer will come to the object at a convenient time for you. He will advise you on types of ceiling, materials, textures and make an accurate measurement of your room, as well as calculate the final cost of the stretch ceiling. The service of calling the technologist-measurer is free of charge. You can make an appointment for measurement with your personal manager by phone or online ordering.
The third step - conclusion of the contract The contract can be concluded in the offices of the company and at your home or on site. At the conclusion of the contract is a prepayment of 30%.
Fourth step - installation and final payment On the appointed day of installation our team will come to you in the agreed place and qualitatively perform the installation of stretch ceiling. After installation of the stretch ceiling the final payment is made.
What is a stretch ceiling?
Stretch ceiling is a ceiling structure, which consists of PVC film, profile, technical insert and other components. PVC film is used to make the ceiling according to the drawing, its edge is harpooned. The aluminum profile is fixed to the wall around the perimeter of the room. Due to the harpoon, the cloth is attached to the profile. The cloth is heated with a heat gun and under the influence of temperature it is stretched, this helps to make the surface of the stretch ceiling perfectly flat. PVC films come in different colors and textures (matte, glossy, satin). Thanks to the wide variety of PVC films stretch ceilings become an integral element of decor, complementing the interior of the room. Despite the popularity of stretch ceilings in the modern world, the origins of their appearance lie in ancient times. Back in ancient Rome, ceilings were draped with fabric, selecting the color of the ceiling so that it harmonized with the color of the walls. There is evidence that modern stretch ceilings were invented in Sweden. And the French, subsequently, refined them to perfection and created PVC film. To date, the technology and materials of stretch ceilings have changed and improved significantly. And now, when there are new production facilities, stretch ceilings are available to you in a very short time!
How to care for stretch ceilings?
Stretch ceilings do not require any special care. Thanks to a special anti-static effect, no dust settles on their surface. However, if necessary, the stretch ceiling can be wiped with a special cleaner (e.g. window cleaner with ammonia) or simply with a microfiber cloth. How many centimeters will the stretch ceiling drop from the main ceiling? The stretch ceiling can drop from 6 to 9 centimeters from the main ceiling. This distance includes: a. 3 cm - the height of the profile with which the canvas is stretched. b. 3-4 cm - the distance that can occur due to the unevenness of the main ceiling, while the stretch ceiling will look perfectly even. c. 2-3 cm - this additional distance occurs when installing chandeliers, because all the wiring is hidden under the stretch ceiling. And if it is planned to install spotlights, the ceiling will be lowered to the height of the spotlight. At the individual consultation our managers and surveyors will tell you all the nuances associated with the distance to which the stretch ceiling can be lowered in a particular case.
Ceiling or wallpaper first?
You can wallpaper either before or after the installation of the stretch ceiling. When choosing which method suits you best, consider the pros and cons of each. If you glue wallpaper before you have installed a stretch ceiling, then remember that a few centimeters of wallpaper near the main ceiling will be out of reach. And in the future, if in 3-5 years you want to change the wallpaper, there will be inconvenience in removing them from the wall in the places of junction with the stretch ceiling. You should also take into account that between wallpaper and ceiling installation should be at least 3-4 days for the wallpaper to dry and strengthen. If you decide to glue wallpaper after the installation of the stretch ceiling, then you should be especially careful in the use of tools when gluing wallpaper, so as not to damage and smear the canvas. With this method, the wallpaper should be carefully glued butt to butt with the stretch ceiling.
What to do before installation?
The first thing you need to do before installing a stretch ceiling is to remove all breakable objects from the room, cover furniture and floor surface to avoid their damage. Prepare for the masters a scheme of communications (electrical wiring, heating and water pipes). It is necessary to provide free access for installers to the place of installation of the ceiling (remove all items of furniture or free space around the perimeter of the room at a distance of at least 1.5 meters from the walls). It is necessary to suspend in the room where the installation of stretch ceilings, all other repair work for the period of work on the installation of stretch ceilings in order not to disrupt the technological process and timing of work. When preparing the surface of the main ceiling before installing a stretch ceiling is enough to clean it from dirt and peeling putty, so that later the debris did not fall on the canvas. It is not necessary to level the main ceiling.
How much does 1m2 cost?
The price of each square meter of stretch ceiling is individual, as well as the desires of our customers! In order to know the cost of the desired stretch ceiling, it is necessary to take into account the type of fabric, all accessories and, most importantly, the specifics of your room (type, quality of walls, stage of finishing, difficult accessibility of installation) The cost is also affected by your chosen cornice and the number of lights. The easiest way to find out the price of your ceiling is to call your personal manager or call a measuring technician.
Why might the price be different?
Sometimes it happens that the price announced by the personal manager during consultation on the phone does not coincide with the final price, which was announced by the technologist after measuring. This happens because the technologist-measurer, having made an accurate measurement of your ceiling, revealed technical nuances and features of the room, which were not mentioned to the manager. For example, in the process of consultation with the technologist-measurer you may decide to install the cornice not on the stretch ceiling, and make it hidden. Or you decide to change the number of lights and their type. Of course, you have the right to change your decision on the type of stretch ceiling, choose a different canvas or decide to make a two-level ceiling, which will entail a difference in price, named by the manager and the measuring technologist.
Which lighting fixtures are suitable for stretch ceilings?
You can choose any lights and chandeliers that suit your interior. However, there are a few rules and guidelines to follow when choosing light bulbs. 1. If you use incandescent lamps in the chandelier (type of base E14, E27), and the plafonds look up, then hang the chandelier should not be closer than 30 cm from the canvas. If this is impossible due to the design, the temperature of the lamps should not exceed 70 ° C. 2. If you use energy-saving lamps or LED lamps (type of base E14, E27) and the distance of the plafonds from the cloth is less than 30 cm, the temperature of the lamps should also not exceed 70°C. 3. If you use spotlights, you can place them at any distance from the canvas, as well as without fear of mounting them in the stretch ceiling - they do not heat up and do not spoil the canvas. 4. If in chandeliers and lamps you use halogen lamps (type of base G4, G9), their temperature should not exceed 40 ° C. 5. Important! Do not use halogen lamps in spotlights with MR16 base, as they strongly heat the canvas from the inside.
Are stretch ceilings harmful to health?
ALFASTYLE stretch ceilings are absolutely safe and do not harm your health. We offer our clients stretch ceilings of BAUF brand - the best world manufacturer. Ceilings of this brand are made of materials that do not emit harmful substances, do not accumulate static electricity. Thanks to these properties they can be installed in almost any room. The safety of our materials is also confirmed by certificates of conformity. View here
How long do ceilings smell, is the odor harmful?
The odor of stretch ceilings after installation can last up to a week. It is harmless, it is confirmed by certificates of conformity and hygiene. The odor appears immediately after installation, because the material is new and, in addition, installers heat the canvas with a heat gun. After installing the stretch ceiling, it is necessary to ventilate the room.
